First Visit
First-Time Visitors
First-time patients are always welcome. We will guide you through every step, from reception to payment.
1
📞
Call or Walk In
Walk-ins welcome. Calling ahead helps ensure a smoother visit.
2
📋
Fill Out Intake Form
We will provide a form at reception. Please fill in your symptoms and current medications.
3
🩺
Consultation
The doctor will conduct a thorough interview and examination. Feel free to share any concerns.
4
💊
Payment & Prescription
Most prescriptions (including Kampo) are dispensed on-site.
🎒 Please bring your My Number Card or eligibility confirmation document, any medical certificates, and your medication notebook. Having information about your current medications will help the consultation go smoothly.
