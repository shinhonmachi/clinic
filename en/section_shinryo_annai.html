Kampo Medicine
Kampo Medicine

Kampo medicine views the body as a whole rather than treating individual parts, aiming to correct the constitutional imbalances at the root of your symptoms. This department is especially recommended for those who felt little effect from Western medications, are concerned about side effects, or want to address long-standing constitutional issues at their source.

At our clinic, we place great importance on the consultation. We carefully inquire about your constitution, physical condition, diet, sleep, lifestyle, and stress levels, and build a prescription tailored specifically to you. Even for the same symptom like "cold limbs," the appropriate Kampo medicine differs greatly depending on one's constitution. Seemingly contradictory symptoms — such as feeling cold yet prone to sweating, or tired yet unable to sleep — can often be clarified through a Kampo perspective, revealing a path to the right prescription.

Kampo can be used alongside Western medicine. If you are currently taking other medications, please do not hesitate to consult us. By combining the strengths of both Kampo and Western medicine in an integrative approach, we help draw out your body's innate healing power.

Psychosomatic Medicine
Psychosomatic Medicine

Psychosomatic medicine specializes in physical symptoms triggered by stress and psychological factors — such as headaches, stomach pain, palpitations, fatigue, and insomnia. If you've been wondering "maybe it's all in my head" or "maybe it's not serious enough to see a doctor," we encourage you to come in sooner rather than later.

Depression, anxiety, and loss of motivation — if left unaddressed, these can significantly impact your daily life and work. At the same time, many patients recover when they receive the right support at the right time. We understand that seeking help can feel like a big step, which is why we take ample time from your very first visit and create an atmosphere where you can talk freely.

Beyond medication, we work together with you on improving your daily rhythm and how to manage stress. We respect each patient's pace and aim for recovery in a way that feels sustainable. Even if you simply feel you need someone to talk to, please don't hesitate to come in.

Internal Medicine
Internal Medicine

We provide broad care including long-term management of lifestyle diseases such as hypertension, diabetes, dyslipidemia, and gout. From children to elderly patients, we serve as your local family doctor.

Lifestyle diseases often progress without noticeable symptoms. If you've been told your blood pressure is a little high but have left it untreated, or if you've had an abnormal health checkup but haven't had time to visit a doctor — please come in sooner rather than later. We will conduct appropriate tests such as blood tests, urinalysis, and ECG as needed, and clearly explain your current condition before recommending a treatment plan you can realistically maintain.

We also welcome consultations about vague symptoms that are hard to pin down in tests — such as general lethargy, persistent fatigue, or ongoing headaches. We carefully investigate the background of your symptoms, and when appropriate, we can combine Kampo or nutritional therapy to address them.
