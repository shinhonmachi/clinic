Body Checker
Measure autonomic nerve balance, stress, and vascular age in about 3 minutes
Body Checker is a testing device that simply attaches a sensor to your fingertip to measure autonomic nervous balance, physical stress, mental stress, peripheral blood circulation, and vascular age in about 3 minutes.

It is easy to operate via a touch panel, and patients can even take the measurement themselves while waiting. Results are printed instantly from the built-in thermal printer, so you can take the figures home with you. We encourage anyone who feels generally unwell or wants to know the state of their autonomic nervous system to give it a try.
📊
Four Main Measurement Items
Autonomic Nervous System & Vascular Age

Based on an analysis of heart rate variability (pulse wave), the test comprehensively evaluates autonomic nerve function and vascular condition. There is no blood draw or painful procedure involved — the measurement is completed simply by attaching a clip-type sensor to your fingertip.

⚖️
Autonomic Nervous Balance
Evaluates the balance between the sympathetic and parasympathetic nervous systems, quantifying which is dominant and visualizing any autonomic imbalance.
💪
Physical Stress
Measures the degree of physical and physiological stress on the body, helping identify signs of overwork, lack of sleep, or poor physical condition.
🧠
Mental Stress
Evaluates the degree of psychological and emotional stress, allowing an objective understanding of how stress from work or relationships affects the autonomic nervous system.
🫀
Peripheral Circulation & Vascular Age
Evaluates capillary blood flow and vessel stiffness at the fingertip, displaying the result as a "vascular age" compared to your actual age. Useful for understanding arteriosclerosis risk.

Test Procedure
Completed in about 3 minutes — results printed on the spot

No pain or blood draw is required at any point.

1
The measurement is started via touch panel. Staff will enter basic information such as your name and age.
2
A clip-type sensor is attached to your fingertip. The pulse wave is measured for about 3 minutes while you rest quietly.
3
Once the measurement is complete, results are automatically printed from the built-in thermal printer. You can take the figures home with you.
4
If you have any questions or concerns about your results, feel free to discuss them with the doctor during your consultation.

🌿 About Using Your Results

The Body Checker results are a reference indicator for understanding the state of your autonomic nervous system and blood vessels. They do not constitute a definitive medical diagnosis, but if you are concerned about the numbers or wish to track changes over time, please consult a doctor.

We can also suggest combining this with treatments aimed at improving autonomic balance and blood flow, such as Super Lizer (near-infrared therapy) or placenta injections. Please feel free to start with the test itself.
